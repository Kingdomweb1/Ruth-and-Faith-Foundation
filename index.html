About Us

We are committed to building access, support, and dignity for women and the girl child.

Ruth and Faith Foundation is a purpose-driven platform focused on helping women and the girl child reach their full potential by promoting equality, social inclusion, and integration across all levels of society.

We exist to create practical opportunities that enable women to engage, express their strengths, and thrive with confidence, dignity, and support. Our work is centered on meaningful transformation, community growth, and lasting impact.

Education

We promote learning opportunities that help women and girls grow in knowledge, confidence, and purpose.

Empowerment

Health Care

We advocate for healthy living and improved access to essential care and wellbeing support.

Training

We provide skill-based development and growth opportunities that build confidence and capacity.

Business Supports

We encourage financial growth and enterprise support that helps women build sustainable livelihoods.
